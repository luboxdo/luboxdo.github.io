 FAVORITE BRAND, DOC MARTENS   

 

 When looking for inspiration for my website, I realized that Doc Martens matched the aesthetic I wanted to mimic best. Doc Martens has been one of my favorite brands for a while. After I got my first pair of shoes from them, this past December, my love for them only grew. They provide extremely high-quality leather shoes in a variety of different sizes. They also put a lot of work into their marketing, diversity, and advertising which I can respect deeply. Doc Martens became my favorite due to two factors. The first is they offered stylish, ethical, good quality, alternative footwear. Which is relatively difficult to find and something I try to look for in most of my products. The second reason is that they had a wide variety of products to choose from. Doc Martens became popularized for their classic leather boot, however, they have something for everybody.  

Why this company?

 Aside from the major reasons I enjoy the company, I also enjoy their aesthetic. Yellow is my favorite color, so I love the use of it in most of their ads – I also think it’s a great use of color in their usual greyscale ads to draw in the consumer. Their website has a lot of bells and whistles but is relatively easy to navigate. Along with their wide variety of inventory, their website is aesthetically pleasing. One of my favorite aspects is that most of their ads and photos they use on their website are monochromatic, with black and white being most of the picture, and (usually) a pop of yellow comes in to draw you into it. 

What do I plan to use?

 I am a huge fan of the font styles and bold colors on their website and want to use them as inspiration for my website. I also enjoy the chunkier font they have, which matches the aesthetic of their shoes. I also want to mimic the layout and style, a white background with black text and yellow accents. I would like to use a combination of Ariel Black and Bernard MT Condensed to mimic the style of their fonts. 

My logo Header 

Nothing is set in stone for my logo header yet, but I would like to keep it relatively simple and brightly colored, similar to Doc Martens website. I want to accent my website the same way I would accent the header, with bright yellow accents. My goal is to keep the website as monochromatic yet stylish as possible.
